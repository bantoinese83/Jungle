# Content Marketing Strategy

## Core Positioning

**Main Message**: "The Critical 5-Minute Window: How AI Can Revolutionize Your Lead Follow-Up Speed"

**Target Audience**:
- Sales leaders and VPs
- Agency owners
- Real estate professionals
- Home service businesses
- B2B SaaS companies

## Hub-and-Spoke Content Model

### Hub Content (Long-form)

**Primary Blog Post**: "The Critical 5-Minute Window: How AI Can Revolutionize Your Lead Follow-Up Speed"

**Key Sections**:
1. The Problem: Why 5 minutes matters
2. The Statistics: Data on lead response times
3. The Solution: AI-powered speed to lead
4. Case Studies: Real results (hypothetical initially)
5. Implementation Guide: How to get started
6. Call to Action: Free trial signup

**SEO Keywords**:
- Primary: "speed to lead", "AI sales caller", "lead qualification automation"
- Secondary: "GoHighLevel AI integration", "automated lead follow-up", "AI phone calls"
- Long-tail: "how to improve sales lead response time", "best AI caller for sales"

### Spoke Content (Repurposed)

1. **X/Twitter Thread** (5-7 tweets)
   - Hook: "Most businesses lose 78% of leads in the first 5 minutes..."
   - Points: Problem, solution, benefits, CTA
   - Visuals: Dashboard screenshots, infographics

2. **LinkedIn Post**
   - Professional tone
   - Focus on ROI and business benefits
   - Target: Sales leaders, agency owners
   - Include link to full blog post

3. **YouTube Short/TikTok** (60 seconds)
   - Before/after comparison
   - Animated explainer
   - Showcase product features
   - Strong CTA

4. **Reddit Posts**
   - r/sales: "How I automated lead follow-up"
   - r/entrepreneur: "AI tool that increased my conversion by 10x"
   - r/gohighlevel: "Integrating AI calling with GHL"

5. **Email Newsletter**
   - Weekly tips on lead management
   - Case studies
   - Product updates

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Schedule

**Monday**: Blog post published
**Tuesday**: LinkedIn post + X thread
**Wednesday**: YouTube Short/TikTok
**Thursday**: Reddit engagement
**Friday**: Email newsletter

### Monthly Themes

- **Month 1**: Speed to Lead Fundamentals
- **Month 2**: CRM Integration Deep Dives
- **Month 3**: Case Studies & Results
- **Month 4**: Advanced Automation

## AI Tools Integration

### Content Creation
- **Jasper/Copy.ai**: Blog outlines, paragraphs, social captions
- **Midjourney/DALL-E**: Visuals, infographics, screenshots
- **Synthesys AI/HeyGen**: Video content, explainer videos

### Optimization
- **Perplexity/ChatGPT**: Research, content ideas
- **Ahrefs/SEMrush**: Keyword research
- **Google Search Console**: Performance tracking

## Distribution Channels

1. **Owned Media**
   - Blog (SEO optimized)
   - Email newsletter
   - Social media profiles

2. **Earned Media**
   - Reddit communities
   - LinkedIn groups
   - Industry forums

3. **Paid Media**
   - Google Ads (search)
   - LinkedIn Ads (B2B)
   - X/Twitter Ads (awareness)

## Conversion Funnel

1. **Awareness**: Blog post, social content
2. **Interest**: Case studies, demos
3. **Consideration**: Free trial, pricing page
4. **Action**: Sign up, book demo

## Metrics to Track

- Blog traffic (organic search)
- Social engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Email open rates
- Trial signups
- Conversion rate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
